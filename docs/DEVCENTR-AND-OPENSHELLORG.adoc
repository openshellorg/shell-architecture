= DevCentr and OpenShellOrg
:toc: preamble

== Division of labor

| Actor | Role |
|---|---|
| **DevCentr** | Recommends and configures developer environments — including recommending Nushell where structured pipelines matter. |
| **OpenShellOrg** | Standardizes (SOS) and builds companion tooling / explanation (this repo, `nu-require`, etc.). |

== Collaboration, not duplication

DevCentr should not fork or reimplement Nushell. OpenShellOrg should not become a second DevCentr. Docs and recommendations can cross-link: DevCentr points practitioners at Nushell and SOS; OpenShellOrg documents the architectural thesis and ships small libs that make that thesis usable.
